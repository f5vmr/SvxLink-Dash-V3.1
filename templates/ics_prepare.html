ICS Hardware Preparation

Prepare the Raspberry Pi for ICS hardware by checking I²C, selecting the correct overlay, and confirming that the required overlay files are installed.

ICS boards require Raspberry Pi I²C and the correct device-tree overlay before the MCP23017 GPIO expanders can be detected. If the overlay is changed, the Raspberry Pi must be rebooted before GPIO verification can continue.
