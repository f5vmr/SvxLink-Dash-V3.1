Port Courtesy Tones

Configure courtesy tone behaviour for each enabled ICS port.

Repeater ports normally need courtesy tone decisions. Simplex ports can usually be left with courtesy tones disabled unless a roger tone is wanted.
Shared tone settings:
Courtesy sound, repeater idle tone and repeater shutoff tone are currently shared by all enabled ports. Settings selected for the first configured port will therefore also apply to every subsequent port.
